About MINTKARD

MINTKARD is a active learning spaced-repition platform, which mean it tests your knowledge just about when you will normally forget it. If you test knowledge too early then that's useless revision, if it's too late then you would forget the content and have to re-understand it. Mintkard uses it's an algorithim to determine that time gap on when to review it next.

A big feature in Mintkard, is the gamification aspect, to make its users love spending time revising and achieve their goals.

"Spaced repetition is to the mind what exercise is to the body. It systematically trains and strengthens the connections between neurons, gradually building durable and efficient pathways for retrieving and using information. By spacing out study sessions and gradually increasing the intervals between them, you can embed new knowledge more deeply and retrieve it more easily over time"

Do you find it hard to retain the information you study? Are you looking for a way to excel academically? Our spaced repetition flashcard app may be the answer you're looking for! With our app, you have the option to create your own flashcards or select from a variety of pre-made decks covering different subjects. We utilize the proven technique of spaced repetition to help you remember what you learn. As you review your flashcards, the app will present the cards you have difficulty with more frequently, gradually increasing the intervals between reviews as you become more comfortable with the material. Don't let a lack of retention hold you back any longer. Sign up for our app and take charge of your academic success!
