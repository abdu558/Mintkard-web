About MINTKARD

MINTKARD is a active learning spaced-repition platform, which mean it tests your knowledge just about when you will normally forget it. If you test knowledge too early then that's useless revision, if it's too late then you would forget the content and have to re-understand it. Mintkard uses it's an algorithim to determine that time gap on when to review it next. 

A big feature in Mintkard, is the gamification aspect, to make its users love spending time revising and achieve their goals.
